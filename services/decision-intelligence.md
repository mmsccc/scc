      - title: "Layer 4: Retrieval"
        description: "AI-native search with three modes: direct query ('what did we decide about billing?'), contextual suggestion (surfaces prior decisions when a new Slack thread starts on the same topic), and onboarding digest (new hire gets their domain's decision history on day one)."
